David Guetta

David Guetta is a renowned French DJ, record producer, and songwriter who has played a significant role in the evolution of electronic dance music (EDM). He was born on November 7, 1967, in Paris, France. Guetta's full name is Pierre David Guetta.

Martin Garrix

LMartin Garrix, whose real name is Martijn Gerard Garritsen, is a Dutch DJ, record producer, and musician. He was born on May 14, 1996, in Amstelveen, Netherlands. Garrix gained worldwide recognition at a young age and quickly became one of the most successful electronic music artists in the industry.

Steve Aoki

Steve Aoki is a renowned American DJ, record producer, and entrepreneur who has made significant contributions to the electronic dance music (EDM) industry. Born on November 30, 1977, in Miami, Florida, Steve Hiroyuki Aoki grew up in a musically inclined family, as his father, Rocky Aoki, was the founder of the Benihana restaurant chain, and his mother, Chizuru Kobayashi, was a former Olympic wrestler.
