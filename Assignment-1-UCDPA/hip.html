Juice Wrld

Juice Wrld, born Jarad Anthony Higgins on December 2, 1998, was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He rose to prominence in the music industry with his introspective lyrics, melodic style, and unique blend of rap and emo influences. Unfortunately, his life was cut tragically short at the age of 21 on December 8, 2019, due to a drug-related seizure.

The Kid Laroi

The Kid Laroi, whose real name is Charlton Kenneth Jeffrey Howard, is an Australian rapper, singer, and songwriter. He was born on August 17, 2003, in Waterloo, New South Wales, Australia. The Kid Laroi gained international recognition and success at a young age, emerging as one of the prominent figures in the contemporary rap and hip-hop scene.

Kid Cudi

Kid Cudi, whose real name is Scott Ramon Seguro Mescudi, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor. He was born on January 30, 1984, in Cleveland, Ohio. Cudi gained prominence in the music industry with his unique style, blending elements of hip hop, rock, and alternative music. His introspective lyrics and vulnerable delivery have resonated with audiences and made him a beloved figure in the industry.
